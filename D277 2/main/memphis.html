The Blues City

While Nashville is known more for Country Music, Memphis is known for blues, rock, and soul. Beale Street is
a historical street for Blues music. Famous Memphis musicians are Elvis Presley, Al Green, Isaac Hayes and Justin
Timberlake. Memphis is also known for awesome barbecue. Memphis barbecue has a sweet, tangy, smoked flavor.

Memphis Demographics

With a population of around 655,000, Memphis is the second largest city in Tennessee.

  1. The city of Memphis was incorporated in 1826.
  2. Memphis is located in the Western part of Tennessee.
  3. The city is classified as urban.
  4. The average income level of Memphis is around $68,226.
