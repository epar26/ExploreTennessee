Music City

Nashville is the capitol of Tennessee and is located in Middle Tennessee. It is also known as "Music City".
Nashville was originally named "Nashborough" and was incorporated in 1806. It was called
Nashborough due to the name of the fort that was located there in 1779. Previous to settlers, the area was
home to the Mississippian culture to around 1400 AD. Later, the area was home to the Cherokee, Chickasaw, and
Shawnee peoples.
